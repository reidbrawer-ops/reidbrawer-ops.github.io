Redwood City

Red Morton Park is the one stop in Redwood City, but it's really two venues in one complex: four lighted outdoor courts anyone can walk onto, and three indoor courts in the Senior Center next door with a single weekly window. Across town, the Sequoia YMCA runs a small beginner-friendly indoor session twice a week.

Four lighted outdoor courts in a shared park complex that also has a skatepark, tennis courts, soccer fields, and basketball courts (Courts A–D). General pickleball open play runs Mon–Sat 8am–12pm and Sun 9am–1pm on all four courts, plus every day 4pm–8pm on Courts A & B only (until the game ends or after 15 minutes if others are waiting). Courts C & D switch to mixed pickleball/tennis use Mon–Sat 12pm–4pm and Sun 1pm–4pm (1-hour limit if others are waiting), and every day 4pm–8pm — Tennis Courts 1 & 2 are also available for pickleball during that midday mixed-use window. Tennis Courts 3 & 4 are tennis-only every day. Reservations go through the city's ActiveCommunities portal.
