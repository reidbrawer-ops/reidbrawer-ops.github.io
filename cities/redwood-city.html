Redwood City

Red Morton Park is the one stop in Redwood City, but it's really two venues in one complex: four lighted outdoor courts anyone can walk onto, and three indoor courts in the Senior Center next door with a single weekly window. Across town, the Sequoia YMCA runs a small beginner-friendly indoor session twice a week.

Four lighted outdoor courts in a shared park complex that also has a skatepark, tennis courts, soccer fields, and basketball courts. Open daily dawn to dusk, with lighted courts playable until 10pm. Secondary sources describe it as public drop-in, first-come-first-served play, but whether Redwood City's ActiveCommunities system offers a reservation option for these courts wasn't confirmed — worth a call before assuming it's walk-up only.
