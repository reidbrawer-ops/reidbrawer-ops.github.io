Daly City

Daly City has five venues worth knowing, but three carry real question marks: Gellert Park isn't officially documented as a pickleball spot, Westlake Park Community Center (Pacelli Gym) has an unsettled court count, and Bayshore Community Center has no confirmed court count at all. The other two are solid — War Memorial Community Center's three indoor courts, and the members-only Bay Club South San Francisco with six dedicated courts.

War Memorial Community Center

Mixed levels
6655 Mission St, Daly City, CA 94014 Directions ↗
3 courts Level: Not specified Hours: Multiple sessions weekly (rotates)
SurfaceMedium — permanent lines, but portable nets and rotating Open Gym scheduling
LevelNot specified
WeatherFoggy, windy — San Bruno Gap microclimate, typical even in summer
WaitMedium — scheduled Open Gym sessions, mixed reservability; no crowding reports either way

Three indoor courts host rotating Open Gym pickleball — Tuesday/Thursday mornings, a Wednesday evening slot co-hosted with the Golden Gate Pickleball Club, and a winter Sunday program — on permanent lines with portable nets. Pricing is genuinely unclear: you'll see it listed as free, the city's fee schedule sets a $6–$7.50 drop-in fee plus a separate $20–$25 court-reservation fee, and the Wednesday session has been quoted at $4 specifically. Confirm the current price and the live schedule through the city's iplay activity calendar before heading over.

2 courts Level: Not specified Hours: 7am–6pm daily
SurfaceMedium — this is officially a tennis court; pickleball lines and nets aren't confirmed
LevelNot specified
WeatherFoggy, windy — San Bruno Gap microclimate, typical even in summer
WaitLow — reservable in advance, no crowding signal

Gellert Park is officially two lighted, reservable tennis courts (7-day advance booking, 7am–6pm), and the city doesn't document it as a pickleball venue — yet it's widely listed as a 2-court pickleball spot with dedicated lines and nets. The most likely explanation is informal shared play on the tennis courts that the city simply hasn't formalized as a pickleball amenity, but that's unconfirmed. Worth a call before making a special trip.

6 dedicated courts Level: Not specified Hours: Mon–Fri 8am–10pm, Sat–Sun 8am–8pm
SurfaceHigh — dedicated indoor pickleball courts in a members-only tennis & pickleball facility
LevelNot specified
WeatherIndoors — no weather factor
WaitLow — membership-required club, reservable courts

Despite the name, Bay Club South San Francisco actually sits in Daly City, near the Cow Palace. Six dedicated indoor courts inside a 90,000+ sq ft tennis-and-pickleball facility, open to members only; pickleball itself carries no extra per-session fee once you're a member, unlike tennis. Membership pricing isn't published — Bay Club directs prospective members to its online membership builder or a call to the club.

Westlake Park Community Center (Pacelli Gym)

3 courts (unsettled — you may see 5) Level: Not specified Hours: Tue/Thu 11am–2pm (reported)
SurfaceMedium — permanent lines in a shared gym; net type and total court count are unsettled
LevelNot specified
WeatherIndoors — no weather factor
WaitLow — narrow contact-based weekly window suggests a small program

This one venue goes by two names — "Pacelli Event Center" and Westlake Park Community Center (officially "WLCC - Pacelli Gym - Westlake Park" in the city's ActiveCommunities system, facility_id=82) — but it's the same gym. The court count is unsettled: one listing claims 5 courts (2 outdoor + 3 indoor, bring-your-own net), while the more reliable picture is 3 indoor courts with nets provided, which is what we go with here. Pickleball hours are Tuesday/Thursday 11am–2pm at $4/session (contact Terry Chew, 650-270-9028) — separate from a Sunday badminton program at the same gym — though the city's fee schedule lists different citywide drop-in and reservation fees, so confirm the actual price before going.

Bayshore Community Center

Courts: Not specified Level: Not specified Hours: Weekdays, mornings–early afternoon (varies by day)
SurfaceLow — surface, net type, and lights all unspecified
LevelNot specified
WeatherIndoors — no weather factor
WaitMedium — active local club, but obscure to outsiders and easy to miss

Bayshore Community Center is the least-documented entry in this guide: there's no published court count, surface, or net type anywhere. What we can confirm is that the city's ActiveCommunities system lists a weekday Badminton/Pickleball open-gym schedule here, and a local "Bayshore Pickleball Club" runs an active group describing itself as 5+ years old with 70+ members. Real, but genuinely under-documented — call the center directly (650-991-5701) to confirm court count and current fees before visiting.

  • Gellert Park, Westlake Park Community Center (Pacelli Gym), and Bayshore Community Center are flagged unconfirmed above — call ahead before a special trip to any of the three.
  • Pricing is inconsistent at several city-run venues — the FY2027 Master Fee Schedule sets a $6.00 resident/$7.50 non-resident Open Gym drop-in fee and a separate $20/$25 court-reservation fee, even though these sessions are often listed as free.
  • War Memorial's Wednesday evening session (7–9pm, Upper Gym) is co-hosted with the Golden Gate Pickleball Club.
  • Bay Club South San Francisco is members-only; the other four venues are city-run and open to the public. For live open-gym schedules, check the city's ActiveCommunities/iplay system directly (Recreation Dept. general line: 650-991-8001).
