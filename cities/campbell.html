South Bay region

Campbell

Two indoor-only venues — no outdoor pickleball courts anywhere in the city. The HUB Silicon Valley is a 20-court membership club, and the Community Center runs a smaller Adult Open Gym session on portable nets, with a court count that shifts between school year and summer.

Twenty individually fenced indoor courts in a 57,000-square-foot former Fry's Electronics store — the only venue of its kind in Campbell. Regular play requires membership, though a $40 all-day guest pass covers walk-ins with in-and-out privileges; courts are booked through CourtReserve. The courts are well-lit and fully indoor. A city planning review of a possible 9-court expansion into adjacent space was underway but hadn't broken ground.

Indoor Adult Open Gym pickleball on portable nets over the hardwood gym floor — not dedicated courts. The city currently runs it in the Auxiliary Gym at 3 courts over summer, shifting to 4 courts in the Main Gym during the school year. Sign-up is mandatory in advance, online or in person, for the single weekly Sunday 6:30–9:45pm session (18+) — no walk-up drop-in, no cash on-site. The session fee is $8; confirm the current time and price before a special trip.

  • The HUB Silicon Valley requires membership or a $40 all-day guest pass; courts are booked through CourtReserve.
  • Campbell Community Center pickleball requires advance sign-up online or in person — no walk-up drop-in, no cash accepted on-site.
  • Community Center court count is seasonal — 4 courts in the Main Gym during the school year, 3 in the Auxiliary Gym over summer (currently the 3-court period).
  • No outdoor pickleball courts were found anywhere in Campbell — both venues here are indoor.
