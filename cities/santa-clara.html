South Bay region

Santa Clara

Two reservation-only venues — no walk-up option at either. The Community Recreation Center at Central Park is the public route: city-run rates, phone check-in, courts shared with tennis. Bay Club Santa Clara is the private route: a members-only club with dedicated pickleball lines and a reputation as one of the busier scenes in the city.

2 venues Reservation required at both

Where to play

Community Recreation Center at Central Park

Beginner-friendly
2625 Hayward Drive, Santa Clara, CA 95051 Get directions →
6 outdoor hard courts $8/hr resident · $10/hr non-resident Level: All levels, beginner lessons available Hours: Open play Mon–Fri 1–3pm, Sat–Sun 8–10pm
SurfaceMedium — blended lines shared with tennis, permanent lines, portable nets
LevelOpen to all levels; structured lesson tiers for beginners (0–1.5, 2.0–2.5)
WeatherHotter, sunnier — inland Santa Clara summers
WaitLow — reservation system, assigned courts

The reservation system is open to residents and non-residents alike. Open play drop-in runs $6 residents/$7 non-residents; reserved courts run $8/hr residents, $10/hr non-residents. Check in by phone (408-247-0178) or in person before heading to your assigned court — this isn't a walk-up venue. Class tiers ("New Learners & Beginners 0–1.5," "Advanced Beginner 2.0–2.5") are lesson levels, not requirements for open play. Open Play for Santa Clara City Residents runs Mon–Fri 1–3pm and Sat–Sun 8–10pm.

A private club — membership required, though guest memberships are available. 10 courts, membership required; court rental packages from $2,000 (2+ hrs reserved time, equipment, and coach included). Programmed times (Monday/Wednesday/Friday evenings, Saturday late morning) are scheduled pickleball sessions, not the club's full hours.

Membership & booking info →
  • Neither Santa Clara venue is walk-up — the Community Recreation Center requires a phone or in-person check-in, and Bay Club requires membership.
  • If you're not a Bay Club member, the Community Recreation Center is the only way to play in Santa Clara proper without joining a club.
  • Bay Club's programmed pickleball windows are narrower than its overall facility hours — call 408-738-2582 before assuming you can play outside those times.
