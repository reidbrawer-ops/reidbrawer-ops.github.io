Pleasanton

Pleasanton's pickleball scene centers on Muirwood Community Park — the Tri-Valley's largest dedicated facility — plus a shared-line tennis park, a Saturday-friendly indoor gym, and two private clubs. The Tri-Valley Pickleball Club is a good resource for current schedules and open play.

The Tri-Valley's largest dedicated pickleball facility: 6 outdoor courts, free and first-come-first-served. Opened November 2022, it draws 40+ players across all skill and age groups on a typical weekend, with a rotating-court system to keep access fair when full. Positive crowd sentiment despite the Saturday rush.

Six indoor courts split between recreational and competitive play, Saturday mornings only (9am–12pm). No reservation needed, but entry requires a pass: $3.75 resident / $4.50 non-resident single visit, plus 6-visit and 15-visit multi-passes. Buy multi-day passes ahead at pleasantonfun.com, or single-day passes in person at the Community Services Dept, 200 Old Bernal Ave (no cash on-site).
