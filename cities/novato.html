Cities / North Bay / Novato

Novato

Two real options, both free and first-come-first-served: Hill Recreation's six courts, and a smaller pair at Pioneer Park.

8 courts 2 parks

Where to play

Hill Recreation Pickleball Courts

Beginner-friendly
1560 Hill Rd, Novato, CA 94947 Directions ↗
6 dedicated courts Level: All levels Hours: 8am–10pm daily
SurfaceHigh — dedicated, permanent lines/nets
LevelAll levels welcome
WeatherMild, marine-influenced — less fog than the coast
WaitHigh — one of Novato's most popular spots, no reservation option

Six dedicated courts behind Novato High, expandable to eight when demand requires, with a paddle-rack rotation once full. Free, first-come-first-served, no way to reserve ahead. The address was recently corrected to 1560 Hill Rd — the previous address on file (500 Crescent Drive) likely belonged to a separate, unrelated facility ("Thigpen Courts"), so the exact map pin isn't fully nailed down yet; call ahead if in doubt.

Pioneer Park

Mixed levels
1007 Simmons Ln, Novato, CA 94945 Directions ↗
2 courts Level: Not specified Hours: Daytime only, no lights
SurfaceMedium — hard surface, net setup unclear (permanent vs. bring-your-own isn't settled)
LevelNot specified
WeatherMild, marine-influenced
WaitLow — smaller, less-known option

A smaller, quieter alternative to Hill Recreation. Free, first-come-first-served, no lights, so no evening play. These are outdoor courts — you may see them listed elsewhere as indoor, but that's almost certainly an error.

  • Neither venue takes reservations — arrival time matters more than planning ahead.
  • Data on Novato's pickleball scene is thinner than most cities in this guide — confirm current status with the city (415-899-8290) before a special trip.
