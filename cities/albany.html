Albany

Ocean View Park is Albany's confirmed, active pickleball venue — four dedicated outdoor courts, free to walk up or reservable ahead. Albany High School's six indoor courts also show up on aggregators, but current operating status is unconfirmed.

Four dedicated outdoor courts with permanent lines and nets, free for walk-up play or reservable ahead for $11/hour resident, $13/hour non-resident (older listings cite lower rates — likely a stale fee schedule, so confirm before booking). The city runs Tuesday Outdoor Pickleball Open Play 4–6pm, and BORP hosts a weekly adaptive pickleball program here (Thursdays and Saturdays) open to players of any skill level. Despite showing up under Berkeley on some listings, this is an Albany city park — Buchanan Street runs along the border, which is the likely source of the mixup.

Six indoor wood courts with permanent lines painted on the gym floor — bring your own net, since none is fixed in place. Pickleheads lists a one-time fee to play, though the only dollar figure found anywhere is a stale 2020 listing citing $5 entry plus $2 paddle rental for a Sunday drop-in session. That same 2020 listing also states the gym was closed for maintenance at the time, and nothing more current confirms whether play ever resumed — Albany's own recreation department page doesn't mention this venue at all, only Ocean View Park. Call ahead to confirm it's actually running before making a special trip.

  • Ocean View Park is reservable ahead via Albany's WebTrac portal ($11/hr resident, $13/hr non-resident) — or just walk up and play for free.
  • BORP runs a weekly adaptive pickleball program at Ocean View Park (Thursdays 5–6:30pm, Saturdays 1–4pm), open to players and volunteers of any age or skill level.
  • The city's Outdoor Pickleball Open Play runs Tuesdays 4–6pm at Ocean View Park, player-led with no instructor.
  • Albany High School's status is unconfirmed — call Albany Recreation & Community Services (510-524-9283) or the school (510-558-2500) before a special trip.
