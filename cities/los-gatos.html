Los Gatos

Four confirmed venues, and only one is free: the town's public courts at La Rinconada Park, a private multi-sport club, a JCC with punchcard access, and a middle school gym reserved for recreation-department club members. Pickleheads credits the city with a fifth location and 23 courts total, but that venue couldn't be independently identified, so treat the citywide total as possibly stale.

Eight dedicated outdoor courts with permanent lines and nets at this private multi-sport club, just north of Netflix's Los Gatos campus. Access requires a Bay Club membership; the club doesn't publish separate pickleball guest-pass or drop-in pricing online, so call ahead if you're not already a member. General club hours run Mon–Fri 5:30am–10pm and Sat–Sun 6am–9pm, though pickleball-specific programmed hours aren't published separately. Amenities include lights, locker rooms, food, and on-site lessons.

The JCC's own site lists six lighted outdoor courts with permanent lines and portable nets, though Pickleheads and Bounce both independently list only four — the JCC's page may simply be more current (its Pickleheads listing dates to 2022, and this venue expanded from just two courts when it opened in 2015). Non-members can buy a 10-visit punchcard for $100, valid six months; clinics run $25–30 and workshops $150–180. Scheduled open play is Tue 8–10am, Thu 4–6pm, Sat 4:30–6:30pm, and Sun 9am–12pm; general facility hours are Mon–Fri 6am–9pm and Sat–Sun 7am–7pm.

Four courts striped onto a single former tennis court in 2018 — the only town-owned, free pickleball courts in Los Gatos, walk-up with no reservations. Bring your own net; there are no lights. This has also been the site of a multi-year noise dispute between players and neighboring residents, including 2025 vandalism (two courts were deliberately oiled) that prompted a $90,000 Town renovation — resurfacing, sound barriers, security cameras, and a time-sensitive locking gate — completed in December 2025. Posted park hours are officially sunrise to one hour after sunset, though Google Maps currently shows an 8am opening, plausibly tied to the new gate.

Three indoor wood-gym courts, BYO net on permanent lines. One aggregator lists three additional outdoor courts at this same address, but that's unconfirmed by any other source and may be conflating separate school courts, so this page counts just the three that Pickleheads and Bounce agree on. Fee structure is unresolved: some aggregators describe a flat one-time fee, but the Los Gatos-Saratoga Recreation Department's own page instead describes access through a $100/year Adult Pickleball Club membership, with weekend Open Play (Sat/Sun 9am–3pm) restricted to members — it's unclear whether a true drop-in option exists separately.
