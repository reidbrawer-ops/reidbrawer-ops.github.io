Cities / South Bay / Sunnyvale

Sunnyvale

Sunnyvale Tennis Center is the city's flagship — dedicated courts plus tennis courts that convert during scheduled hours, both bookable ahead. Sunnyvale Community Center offers a smaller indoor drop-in option, but call ahead to confirm hours. The Tennis Center's exact court count is unsettled, so treat any single number as approximate.

Six indoor courts, drop-in for $5/session or $45/year. Listed hours are Mondays 10am–1pm only, but the session may be suspended — status unconfirmed, so call (408) 730-7350 to verify.

  • Las Palmas Park (800 Spinosa Dr) may have courts, but we couldn't confirm them — call the city at (408) 732-2130 before a special trip.
  • Weekday evenings and weekends at the Tennis Center are hardest to book — plan ahead if that's your only window.
