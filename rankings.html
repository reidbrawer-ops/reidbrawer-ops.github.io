Vote for a favorite and rate any court — no login needed. Rankings update live as votes come in, grouped by city so you can see what your own local scene actually thinks.

How ranking works

Where we have a venue's Google rating, we show it alongside for reference — never blended into this score. No court can pay to rank higher — there's nothing to buy. Venues with zero ratings sit outside the list until someone rates them.

Rated on
  • Beginner friendliness
  • Court surface
  • Wait time
  • Advanced play
  • Amenities
  • Atmosphere
Badges, and how this differs from Top pick

A court's overall rating is the average of its scores across those six factors, and a court needs a handful of votes on each before it can earn a ★ Top rated badge — so one enthusiastic vote can't tag a court on day one. This is community voting, and it's separate from the editorial Top pick on each city page: that's our own starting-point recommendation (rated on surface, level, weather, and wait), never a paid placement and never driven by these votes — though a city's Top pick shifts to the highest community-rated court once enough people weigh in.
