Community rankings

Vote for your favorite court.

Every court on this site can be voted for as a favorite and rated on six factors — beginner friendliness, court surface, wait time, advanced play, amenities, and atmosphere. No login needed. Rankings update live as votes come in, grouped by city so you can see what your own local scene actually thinks.

Where available, each court also shows its public Google rating (e.g. "Google 4.3★ (128)") right next to the community rating above. These are two separate numbers from two different populations of people — we don't average them into one score.
