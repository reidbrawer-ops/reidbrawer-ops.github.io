Privacy policy

What this site collects (not much).

Pickleball Bay Area is a static, no-account site. This page explains the little data that passes through it, in plain language.

Hosting

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub, not us, receives standard server logs for every request (IP address, browser type, page requested) as part of running the hosting infrastructure. We don't have access to that log data ourselves. See GitHub's own privacy statement for what they do with it.

No accounts, no analytics, no ad trackers

There's no login, no user accounts, and — as of this writing — no analytics script, tracking pixel, or ad network running on this site. We don't set any cookies of our own.

The correction form

The correction form is handled by a third-party form service, Formspree. If you submit a correction, the details you enter (and your email address, if you choose to include one so we can reply) are sent through Formspree to us. We use it only to review and act on the correction — we don't add you to a list or share it elsewhere. See Formspree's privacy policy for how they handle that data in transit.

Outbound links

This site links out to city recreation department pages, booking systems, retailers, and other third-party sites (for example, on the gear & rentals page). Once you click through, you're subject to that site's own privacy practices and cookies, not ours. Some outbound links may in the future be affiliate links, meaning we could earn a small commission if you make a purchase after clicking — if and when that's the case, it'll be disclosed on the page where the link appears, and this policy will be updated to match.

Children's privacy

This site isn't directed at children and doesn't knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

If what this site collects or how it's shared changes, this page will be updated to reflect that, with a new date below.

Last updated: July 2026. Questions about this policy can be sent through the correction form.
