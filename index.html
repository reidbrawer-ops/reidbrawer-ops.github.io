How this stays honest.

What we check

Every venue is cross-referenced against a real source — its city's own recreation department page or facility directory wherever one exists, and the venue's own listing for the private clubs no city directory covers. Not pulled from an app and left unverified.

What we don't do

No ads. No pay-to-list. Nothing sponsors or influences the court data. Some paddle & gear links are affiliate — always disclosed at the link. If a city's scene is thin or contested, we say so.

How to use it

Court counts, hours, and fees move with city budgets and construction. Treat this as a starting point, then check the source link on each city's page. Data verified July 2026.
